Directions to the Church

Our wedding will be held in Wallaceville Church, which is in Whiteman's Valley, Upper Hutt. From Wellington, follow State Highway 2 (signs for Lower Hutt) for about 30 minutes, then take the Wallaceville / Whitemans Valley exit. At the roundabout, take a right onto Fergusson Drive, and then a quick left onto Ward Street. At the end of Ward Street, take a left at the roundabout and then your first right, onto Wallaceville Road. Finally, after about 10 minutes, turn right onto Whitemans Valley Road, and the church will be on your left (number 7).

Directions to the Reception Venue

The reception will be held in Avalon Public Hall, which is in Avalon, Lower Hutt. From Wallaceville Church, head back the way you came to get back on the motorway, going South. After about 15 minutes, take the "Avalon / Stokes Valley" exit. At the roundabout, turn left onto High Street, then you will soon see Mabey Road on your left. The hall is at number 20.
